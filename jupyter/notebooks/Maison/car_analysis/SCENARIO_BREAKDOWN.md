## Key Comparisons

### Upfront Cost Difference (V2H vs No V2H):
- **$7,000 difference**
- This is ONLY the charger difference ($7,900 V2H vs $900 standard)
- Both scenarios include the same $54,995 car and ~$150 net solar cost

### Why Both Include Solar:
Based on your requirements:
- You specified wanting to see solar benefits for EV/PHEV options
- Solar provides ~$1,000/year in energy savings
- Net solar cost after rebates is only ~$150
- Makes economic sense for any EV/PHEV purchase

### Annual Operating Difference (V2H vs No V2H):
- **$87/year** (V2H saves this much in rate arbitrage)
- NOT significant for economic payback
- V2H payback = $7,000 / $87 = 80+ years
- **Real V2H value = backup power**, not savings
